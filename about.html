Profile

I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from the Université de Sherbrooke, Canada, in September 2023, supervised by Éric Marchand. I spent a semester as an inter-university transfer student at McGill University and Concordia University. I hold a BS-MS from IISER Pune (2019).

I’m a member of the AI Career Accelerator Program at Amii (since Sept 2023). Beyond academia, I enjoy playing chess.

Research Interests

Machine Learning; Fairness & Privacy; Uncertainty Quantification; Statistical Decision Theory; Bayesian Statistics; Shrinkage Estimation & Prediction.
